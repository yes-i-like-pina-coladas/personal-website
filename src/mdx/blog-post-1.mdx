Prototyping is one of the most valuable skills a product manager can have. It's the ability to take an idea and turn it into something tangible that people can see and interact with. This is a powerful way to communicate your vision, get feedback, and de-risk your product development process.

In this post, I'll share why I believe every PM should prototype and how you can get started.
